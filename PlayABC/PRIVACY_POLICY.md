## Introduction

PlayABC is an educational app that helps children learn the alphabet through letters, pictures, and games. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use the app.

---

## Data We Do Not Collect

- **No personal information.** We do not collect names, emails, or any other information that identifies you or your child.
- **No account required.** The app does not require sign-in or account creation.
- **No usage or analytics data.** We do not collect how the app is used, how long it is used, or any analytics or diagnostics for tracking.
- **No data shared with third parties.** We do not send any user data to third parties for advertising or any other purpose.

---

## How the App Works

- **On-device only.** All learning content (letters, words, sounds, games) runs on your device. Text-to-speech uses the device’s built-in voice and does not send any data to us or to Apple beyond what the system normally uses.
- **Sound and speech.** The app may play sounds and use the device’s speech synthesis to speak letters and words. No voice or audio is recorded or sent to us.
- **No internet required for core features.** The app works offline for learning and games. If you open a link to this privacy policy or another webpage from the app, that is the only time the app may use the internet (and only if you tap the link).

---

## Children’s Privacy

PlayABC is designed for young children. We do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children. The app does not have user accounts, social features, or advertising.
